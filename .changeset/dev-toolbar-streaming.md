✨ Show in-flight AI calls in the dev toolbar's exchanges tab. Streaming requests now appear at the top of the dropdown with a `streaming…` badge and the response pane renders the partial text live as it arrives — no need to wait for the call to finish to inspect a long-running parse. When the call completes, the live entry is replaced by the normal completed exchange row with token counts and the full structured response.
